# Workflow Screens

## Dashboard

The supply dashboard now surfaces KPI cards, action queue, environment badges, today timeline empty state and the existing operational tables.

## Order Proposals

Proposal UI can reuse the T0/T1/T2/T3 timeline, formula explanation, warning list, decision panel and disabled-action messaging.

## Supplier Orders

Supplier order screens should show workflow progress from draft to receiving, plus export, email, confirmation, transport, logistics and audit panels.

## Emails And AI

Email and AI extraction screens should present AI output as review evidence. Accepting extraction must remain separate from applying business changes.

## Form Autofill

Form autofill review separates extracted, normalized and final values and keeps validation/application as explicit user actions.

## Supplier Confirmations

Confirmation screens should show source, dates, item discrepancies, logistics links and audit history.

## Transport

Transport screens show comparison, scoring explanation and the warning that recommendation is not automatic carrier selection.

## Logistics

Logistics screens use timeline, receiving discrepancy panels, notifications and audit history.

## Pilot, Integrations And Health

Pilot, integration and health screens retain safe-mode warnings, masked credentials and status-oriented review panels.
