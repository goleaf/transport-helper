# Supplier Performance Report

Shows supplier order confirmation, quantity match, lead time and delay quality.

## Required Data

- Supplier orders.
- Supplier confirmations.
- Supplier confirmation items.
- Logistics records.

## Interpretation

High mismatch count or delay count should trigger supplier review.
Low quantity match rate can indicate supplier packing, SKU matching or confirmation quality issues.

## Limitations

The report depends on recorded confirmations and logistics dates.
