# Blockers And NOT COMPLETE

If the task cannot be fully completed, create:

docs/blockers/current-task-blockers.md

Include:

* what failed;
* exact command;
* exact error;
* files affected;
* what was already done;
* what remains;
* what is needed from user.

Final response must say:

NOT COMPLETE

Do not claim success if:

* tests failed;
* scripts failed;
* required files missing;
* acceptance criteria incomplete;
* push failed without reporting;
* blocker exists.
